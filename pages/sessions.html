Sessions

Looking to improve your public speaking and leadership skills? Gain the confidence to speak up? Come and learn with "Speak to Lead" The vast majority of people (75%) rank the fear of public speaking as their number one anxiety –according to the National Institutes of Mental Health. For some people, this means a fear of speaking to large groups. For others, it means speaking to even a single person if that person has the power to evaluate you, as in a supervisor, interviewer, or professor giving an oral exam. “Speak to Lead” is a Public Speaking and Leadership Program, within "C. Negruzzi" High School, that provides experiential workshops and practical opportunities for students to radiate confidence in their own communication and leadership skills for personal growth and social intelligence in a variety of speaking situations.

Empower your public speaking!!!

  • A good speech is like a pencil... it has to have a point ... and we are happily evolving in our journey to sharpen that pencil
  • Listen with curiosity. Speak with honesty. Act with integrity.
  • "Speak to Lead" public speaking workshops - where speaking is about inspiring others !

The ultimate goal of Speak to Lead is to provide the practical opportunity for each student at their own pace to develop their own communication and leadership skills through a positive experience.

We've added a mentorship program to "SPEAK TO LEAD CLUB" _ IPLT "C.Negruzzi" so you can get or give support to another member of our community. I am excited to see us build stronger relationships with one another and hope you'll join. If you haven't paired with anyone yet, we encourage you to reach out! Make sure to check your requests as well.

Venture the world of excellence by mastering the art of communications and leadership skills with "SPEAK TO LEAD"- public speaking workshops !
